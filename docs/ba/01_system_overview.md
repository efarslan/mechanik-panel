# System Overview – Mechanik

## Description
Mechanik is a vehicle service management system designed for automotive repair businesses to manage their operational processes, track service history, and maintain structured records of customers and vehicles.

## Purpose
The system aims to replace manual or fragmented tracking methods (such as paper or spreadsheets) by providing a centralized platform for managing vehicle service operations and historical data.

## Users
- Business Owners
- Service Advisors (Authorized personnel within the business)

> Note: The system is currently designed for internal business use only. End customers do not directly interact with the system.

## System Structure
Users can either:
- Create a new business account
- Join an existing business as an employee using an access code

All data is managed within the context of a business.

## Core Modules
- Vehicle Management
- Customer Management
- Service Management
- Business Management (basic reporting & insights)
- Authentication & Authorization

## Key Capabilities
- Register and manage vehicles within a business
- Track complete service history per vehicle
- Record and monitor service operations
- Maintain structured customer-vehicle relationships
- Provide simple operational insights through basic reporting
