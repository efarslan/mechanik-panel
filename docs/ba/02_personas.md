# Personas – Mechanik

## 1. Business Owner / Admin

### Profile
Business owner or authorized manager responsible for overseeing all operations within an automotive service business.

### Goals
- Track all vehicle service activities
- Maintain historical service records
- Monitor business performance at a high level
- Ensure operational continuity and data accuracy

### Behaviors
- Uses the system primarily for oversight rather than daily operations
- Reviews vehicle history when needed
- Relies on system data for decision making

### Pain Points
- Difficulty tracking historical vehicle maintenance manually
- Lack of centralized record keeping
- Inability to quickly access past service information (e.g., last timing belt change)

## 2. Service Advisor

### Profile
Operational user responsible for managing day-to-day service entries and vehicle records.

### Goals
- Quickly add and update vehicle/service records
- Access vehicle history efficiently
- Ensure accurate data entry during customer interactions

### Behaviors
- Actively uses the system during service operations
- Enters service details in real time
- Searches vehicle history frequently
